Implementation Plan
  1. Accounts master: Cash, bank, UPI/wallet with opening balance.
  2. Category master: Sales, purchase, packing, courier, salary, rent.
  3. Every income/expense/transfer is posted to one finance ledger.
  4. Order payments and expenses sync to selected account/category.
  5. Account-wise balance, total company fund, receivable and P&L reports.
